Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started. Still unsure? Just ask →

Project timelines depend on complexity and package. The Basic Package (branding) typically takes 3–5 business days. The Standard Package takes 5–7 business days. The Premium Package, which includes a website, generally takes 7–14 business days. We strive to get your project done as quickly as possible while delivering the best results.
Each project includes included revision rounds. Logo design includes 2 rounds; website projects include 2–3 rounds. Additional revisions beyond the included rounds are available at a small extra cost. We aim to get it right within included revisions by understanding your brief thoroughly from the start.
All package prices are once-off payments with no hidden fees. The displayed price is what you pay for design and setup work. Note that hosting and domain registration (for websites)and other external costs are third-party costs paid directly to the provider — we advise you on the best provider and assist with setup. We keep everything fully transparent.
We help you set up your own hosting account with a reliable provider — so you own your website fully and pay hosting directly. For ongoing maintenance such as content updates, security, or feature additions, we offer monthly support arrangements. Ask us about monthly support when you enquire.
Having your business information ready speeds up the process. This includes your business name, services or products, preferred colours or style references, contact details, and any existing brand materials. For websites, having your content (text) prepared in advance is helpful — but we guide you through everything step by step.
We accept EFT (bank transfer) and selected digital payment methods. For larger packages, we require a 50% deposit upfront to secure your project slot, with the balance due before final delivery. Full payment details are included in your formal quote.
Absolutely. Many clients start with the Basic or Standard Package and add services as their business grows. When upgrading, you only pay the difference. Our solutions are built to scale — there's no pressure to get everything at once.
Simply fill in the contact form below or send us a WhatsApp message. We respond within 24 hours — usually much sooner — to discuss your needs and send a formal quote. There's no obligation; just a conversation to see how we can help your business grow.
